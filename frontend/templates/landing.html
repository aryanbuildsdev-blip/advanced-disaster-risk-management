How It Works & Core System Features
Artificial Intelligence

The platform operates three custom-trained **Random Forest Classifiers** with high parameter stratifications. They evaluate live coordinates to output risk categories and neural classification probability.

Real-World Weather Sync

Powered by **Open-Meteo’s telemetry API**, clicking points on the global map or searching cities fetches live meteorological variables (precipitation, humidity, wind, and soil moisture) automatically.

WebGL 3D Orbit Simulations

Instead of flat graphics, the physics models render within a **Three.js** canvas. You can click and drag to orbit in 3D, scroll to zoom, and right-click to pan around the simulation grids.

History & Auditing

Creating an account and logging in activates secure, automated **SQLite database logging**. This saves your prediction queries and tracks environmental telemetry for your audits.
